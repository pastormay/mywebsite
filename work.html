My Work

SummerBlast Conference

SummerBlast is a conference that is very dear to my heart. I host it in Chicago every year. It is a meeting where my Pastor, Reverend Victor Adeyemi, and his Pastor, Reverend George Adegboye, have the liberty to minister to people extensively. I also minister at this conference but I sit to get ministered to for the most part. SummerBlast is an 8-day conference starting from Sunday and ending the following Sunday. There is a Minister's Conference on Monday and Tuesday. Saturday is a day for Music and Healing. SummerBlast is the annual convention for KingsWord North America. People travel from all over the United States, Canada and other countries to attend SummerBlast. KingsWord pastors and members come from Africa, Europe and other parts of North America. It is always an exciting time.

See you at the next SummerBlast!

Supernatural Conference

The Supernatural Conference or Supernatural Campmeeting is an annual event that I hold in Lagos, Nigeria. The conference lasts for 8 days with meetings in the morning and evening on most of the days. People come from all over Nigeria to attend the conference. Members of KingsWord Churches from all over the country as well as our partners gather together for this impactful conference. If you want an extended time in God's presence that guarantees change, this is the meeting for you. Real change takes time and we give God all the time He needs to bring the change He wants. I usually have other ministers join me throughout the week to minister at these meetings. Come for an encounter with God.

See you at the next Supernatural Campmeeting!

Grace Culture

Every year I hold the GRACE Culture meeting on the first Monday in October in Lagos, Nigeria. The vision for Grace Culture is to disciple believers in the realities of God's Grace. When Grace becomes a culture then believers will have no problems making it a lifestyle. We have been called by grace, saved by grace and brought into God's kingdom to live by grace. It takes time to adopt a culture. That is why these meetings are extended times in God's Word. A Grace Culture meeting typically starts at 10:00am and ends at 5:00pm. Each season, people leave with fresh revelation about the Grace of God. I usually have at least one other teacher of the Word who is well-versed in the subject of Grace join me for the teachings.

Join me for the next Grace Culture meeting!

SNOW Meeting

SNOW is an acronym for 'Supernatural Night of Wonders.' I host SNOW meetings at the different KingsWord Churches all over the world. They are meetings marked with revelatory teaching of God's Word followed by signs and wonders. I am always amazed at the miracles God does at these meetings. I regularly hold SNOW meetings all over North America, U.K. and Nigeria. I hope you can make plans to join us at one of these SNOW meetings sometime soon. You will have an experience with God that you wont soon forget.

See you at a SNOW meeting sometime soon!

Pray With Dr Kay

Every weekday morning( Monday to Friday), I lead a prayer conference with all our members and partners all over the world. There is no better way to start your day than by prayer. I believe in individual prayer but I also believe there is power when we come together to pray. You can usually connect to this prayer session on my social media pages and also through the KingsWord App. We pray for 15 minutes from 7:00am - 7:15am CST. It is short and powerful and a great way to start your day.

Join me for prayer sometime soon!

KingsWord Apostolic Center

When I am not traveling, you can usually catch me preaching at The KingsWord Apostolic Center in Chicago. We are located in a beautiful cathedral that was built in 1905. I preach there on Friday nights and Sunday mornings. I really enjoy my time with my Chicago family. They are the best congregation one could ask for. I am not the resident pastor of the church but that is the base for my Apostolic ministry. The church was founded in 2002 when my wife and I moved from California to Chicago based on God's direction. From a group of 6 people in a basement, it has grown to become a church in a beautiful facility and a place that many people call home.

Visit us sometime soon!
