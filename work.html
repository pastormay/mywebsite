My Work

See some of what I am working on this year...

Women's Conference

I convene annual Women's Conferences in North America( Chicago), Europe( London) and Africa( Lagos). They are a time for women to get together in fellowship and to hear an encouraging word for the year. I usually invite awesome speakers to join me in ministering at these conferences. We explore a theme each year. The theme is a prophetic word for the year and it conveys a powerful message from God for that season of our lives. The women usually leave more encouraged, more motivated and more energized than before. To find out the conference dates for this year, visit www.kingswordwomen.org.

See you at the next Women's Conference near you!

Women's Retreat

Every December, I have a Refresh, Recharge and Refocus retreat for women. The retreat is in Chicagoland at an exclusive resort just outside the city. The purpose of the retreat is to give women an opportunity to relax and recharge from the curret year in preparation for the next one. Women come from different cities of the United States and Canada to attend these retreats. They enjoy good food, good fellowship and a great tim ein God's Word. We have opportuntities to share testimonies and intimate details about our lives that we maynot be able to at other times during the year. On the Saturday night of the retreat, we have the Pajamas Praise Party. This is always a favorite for the attendees. We just let our hair down, dance and have a great time. The retreat is a registered event. Registration opens in March. For more information, visit www.kingswordwomen.org.

Register for the next Women's Retreat today!

Women In Leadership Seminar

The Women in Leadership Seminar holds every year in Lagos, Nigeria. The vision for the Women In Leadership Seminars is to help women enjoy their work in fulfilling their God-given assignemnt. It is a seminar for female Pastors, Pastor's Wives, female Ministers and all women in ministry leadership to get equipped for the next level in their assignment. It is help the day before the KingsWord Women's Conference in Lagos. For more details about this year's Women In Leadership Seminar, please visit wwww.kingswordwomen.org.

Join us for the next Women in Leadership Seminar!

Strategy - KingsWord

As Executive Vice President of KingsWord Everywhere, my role is to help optimize our existing activities and operations by coming up with strategies that connect our present day to where we see KingsWord in the future. In simple terms, I try to make sense out of what we do and encourage streamlining things that don't serve our purpose. I am currently working on our Mission Churches and optimizing the way we do missions.

See you at a SNOW meeting sometime soon!

Dear Pastor's Wife

I like to encourage Pastor's wives and discuss issues that are important to us. It can be a lonely walk sometimes. The people you see most often may not understand you. Your world may seem small. You may go through things that make you feel alone. 'Dear Pastor's Wife' started as a monthly recording that I put up on facebook and YouTube. It is now chaging to become a monthly podcast. Watch out for it and subscribe to hear nuggets on being a Pastor's wife or just to laugh at some of my stories.

Subscribe to the Dear Pastor's Wife Podcast!

KingsWord Apostolic Place

I have a vision to decorate the KingsWord Apostolic Place in a way that reminds us of our calling to the world and our commitment to that call. Although, other than putting up a few maps of the world, I haven't yet done much with this vision, I plan to do much more as the days get much warmer. I hope to put subtle reminders all over the facility. They will not be obnoxious but will be a pleasant way of staying focused on our calling.

Visit us sometime soon!
