About Me

Let me tell you a few things about myself...

BIO

In August of 1990, the Lord told me these words, 'Raise me a Supernatural army'. I have been running with those words ever since. I started a Christian fellowship on campus in Ile-Ife Nigeria while I was a student there. After graduation, I started a church in Lagos, Nigeria. In 1999, the Lord asked me to move to the United States of America. I started a church in Chicago, Illinois in 2002. These 3 churches have grown to produce many other churches all over Africa, Europe and North America. They are the KingsWord Churches. I am called to be an Apostle of the Lord Jesus Christ. I delight in church planting and traveling the world to preach the Gospel.

KingsWord Churches

KingsWord International Churches, KingsWord Campus Churches, TheNew

The KingsWord Churches all over the world are my heart. I have a responsibility to plant strong, vibrant churches all over the world. By the Grace of God, we have already done this to an extent. We have churches in North America, Europe and Africa. Through these churches, people are being discipled. They are discovering God's purpose for their lives and they are getting equipped to go after that purpose.

My Apostolic Work

My responsibility to the Body of Christ

I feel a deep responsibility towards the body of Christ. I believe this is connected to my calling as an apostle. The word apostle means 'sent one'. I am sent to preach the Word with the demonstration of God's power all over the world. That is why I travel to different nations to preach and teach God's Word. I travel to Asia, Africa, Europe and different cities in North America; preaching the gospel. It brings me great joy to see people get a revelation of God's Word and rise up out of their darkness to embrace the freedom that comes from the truth of the Word of God. I also love to see people healed in their bodies. It is truly a demonstration of God's Word.

My Family

My first and primary call

My most important call is to be a husband and a father. Without the support of my family, I couldn't do what I do. I am blessed with a great wife and two wonderful children. I love them with all my heart and I am committed to them. They bring me so much joy. I can not say enough about their contribution to my life and ministry. My wife's name is Mayowa, people call her Pastor May. My son's name is Tolu, he calls himself Kayson. My daughter's name is Temi. She simply goes by Temi for now.
